|------------------------|
BEATING THE GAME:       It's a Red Jewel hunt! The objective is super simple:|

Find the Red Jewels you need, and talk to the Jeweler. That's it!

Check the Jeweler's inventory to find out how many Red Jewels you need to beat the game.

Happy hunting!
|------------------------|
BEATING THE GAME:       You must do the following two things to beat the game:|

1. RESCUE KARA          Kara is trapped in a painting! You need Magic Dust to free her.|

She can be in either Edward's Prison, Diamond Mine, Angel Village, Mt. Temple, or Ankor Wat.|

You can search for her yourself, or find Lance's Letter to learn where she can be found.|

Once you find Kara, use the Magic Dust on her portrait to free her.|

2. GATHER MYSTIC STATUES Each time you play the game, you may be required to gather Mystic Statues.|

Talk to the teacher in the South Cape classroom to find out which Statues you need.|

Statue 1 is in the Inca Ruins. You need the Wind Melody, Diamond Block, and both Incan Statues.

Statue 2 is in the Sky Garden. You'll need all four Crystal Balls to fight the boss Viper.|

Statue 3 is in Mu. You need both Statues of Hope and both Rama Statues to fight the Vampires.|

Statue 4 is in the Great Wall. You need Spin Dash and Dark Friar to face the boss Sand Fanger.|

Statue 5 is in the Pyramid. You'll need all six Hieroglyphs as well as your father's journal.|

Statue 6 is at the top of Babel Tower. You'll need the Aura and the Crystal Ring to get to the top.|

Alternatively, if you collect enough Red Jewels to face Solid Arm, he can also take you there.|

Once you've freed Kara and gathered the Statues you need, enter any Dark Space and talk to Gaia.|

She will give you the option to face Dark Gaia and beat the game. Good luck, and have fun!|

|------------------------|
EXPLORING THE WORLD:    When you start the game, you only have access to a few locations.|

As you gain more items, you will be able to visit other continents and access more locations.|

Here are some of the helpful travel items you can find in the game:|

- Lola's Letter         If you find this letter, read it and go see Erik in South Cape.|

- The Teapot            If you use the Teapot in the Moon Tribe camp, you can travel by Sky Garden.|

- Memory Melody         Play this melody in Neil's Cottage, and he'll fly you in his airplane.|

- The Will              Show this document to the stable masters in either Watermia or Euro.|

- Large Roast           Give this to the hungry child in the Natives' Village.|

If you're ever stuck in a location, find a Dark Space. Gaia can always return you to South Cape.

|------------------------|
WHAT IF I'M STUCK?       There are a lot of item locations in this game! It's easy to get stuck.|

Here are some resources that might help you:|

- Video Tutorial         Search YouTube for a video guide of this randomizer.|

- Ask the Community     Find the IoGR community on Discord! Someone will be happy to help you.|

- Check the Spoiler Log Every seed comes with a detailed list of where every item can be found.
